About Me

Photographer

Hi! I am a photographer by hobby and an Engineer by profession. I operate from Bangalore but I am ready to travel anywhere in the world to capture good photos.

I have been working in the field of photography for the past 3 years by now and in the field of wedding photography for about a year. The reason why I became a wedding photographer is that it gives me the opportunity to capture all kinds of emotions, feelings, and memories in such a beautiful way.

Furthermore, I have a lot of great experience working in the field, and I can capture weddings, birthdays, corporate events, ceremonies, candids, and portraits. Available for both Photography and Videography.
